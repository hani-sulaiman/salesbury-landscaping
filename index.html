services

services

Garden landscaping services for every need

Enhance your outdoor space with our professional garden landscaping services, customized to suit every need and preference. From design to installation, we create beautiful, functional gardens that reflect your style.

SOFT LANDSCAPE

We offer a fully comprehensive landscape service from concept development through the design of landscape and irrigation systems to construct, operation and ongoing maintenance. We have extensive nursery facilities for the production and establishment of a wide variety of plant species to supply our landscape schemes and to continue the development and variety within out ongoing projects

HARD LANDSCAPE

Hardscape is becoming increasingly popular in garden designs here in United Arab Emirates. Due to the high costs of water and unforgiving environment maintaining grass lawn areas is both problematic and expensive. Using a range of natural stone, slate and other materials Salesbury Landscape can design and build aesthetically pleasing yet low maintenance gardens using Hardscape techniques

IRRIGATION

We Take responsibility for the complete installation of landscaping and irrigation projects. Each construction team consists of qualified managerial, supervisory and technical staff, who are fully experienced in all aspects of hard and soft landscaping and irrigation.

MAINTENANCE

We enforce our stringent quality control policies of the company. Through on-going training and development of our garden staff we seek to enhance their skills and create a more motivated and interested workforce.

WATER FEATURE

Turn your landscaping into a peaceful retreat with the soothing sound and movement of water. Whether it’s a waterfall, fountain, we can help you create a slice of paradise in your own outdoor space.

SWIMMING POOL

Designing a pool that is suitable for both your family and property is key to the long term successful implementation of any new swimming pool project. Using our industry experience Salesbury Landscaping can design and build beautiful pools that are both visually stunning and also practical to use and maintain.

PERGOLAS & GAZEBOS

Extend your living area from indoors to outdoors with a pergola or gazebo could be one of the best investments you’ll ever make. For those with small or large spaces, a pergola or gazebo might mean adding a little shady oasis for others it might mean creating a fire pit, gourmet outdoor kitchen, cabana more than just a patio or deck, outdoor additions can become a favorite gathering spot for family and friends. Whatever you imagine, Salesbury Landscaping can create it.

WOOD DECKING

Our company specializes in designing and constructing high-quality wood decking solutions that enhance outdoor spaces and provide aesthetic appeal, functionality, and durability. With a proven track record of successful wood decking projects, we take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail. 1. Design Expertise 2. Premium Materials 3. Customization Options 4. KIDS PARKS & PLAY EQUIPMENT’S

We are committed to fostering creativity, physical activity, and social interaction through our thoughtfully designed play area and top-quality play equipment. Our dedication to safety and fun ensures that families can enjoy a memorable and worry-free experience at our establishment.
